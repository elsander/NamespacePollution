lspls: LS-PLS Models

Implements the LS-PLS (least squares - partial least squares) method described in for instance Jørgensen, K., Segtnan, V. H., Thyholt, K., Næs, T. (2004) A Comparison of Methods for Analysing Regression Models with Both Spectral and Designed Variables. Journal of Chemometrics, 18(10), 451–464.
