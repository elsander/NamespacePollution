sdmpredictors: Species Distribution Modelling Predictor Datasets

Terrestrial and marine predictors for species distribution modelling from multiple sources, including WorldClim <http://www.worldclim.org/>,, ENVIREM <http://envirem.github.io/>, Bio-ORACLE <http://www.oracle.ugent.be/> and MARSPEC <http://www.marspec.org/>.
