AHMbook: Functions and Data for the Book 'Applied Hierarchical Modeling in Ecology'

Provides functions and data sets to accompany the book 'Applied Hierarchical Modeling in Ecology: Analysis of distribution, abundance and species richness in R and BUGS' by Marc Kery and Andy Royle. The first volume appeared early in 2016 (ISBN: 978-0-12-801378-6, <https://www.mbr-pwrc.usgs.gov/pubanalysis/keryroylebook>; the second volume is in preparation and additional functions will be added to this package.
