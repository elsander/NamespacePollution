mvSLOUCH: Multivariate Stochastic Linear Ornstein-Uhlenbeck Models for Phylogenetic Comparative Hypotheses

Fits multivariate Ornstein-Uhlenbeck types of models to continues trait data from species related by a common evolutionary history.
