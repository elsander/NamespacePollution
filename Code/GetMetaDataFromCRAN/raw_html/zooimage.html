zooimage: Analysis of numerical zooplankton images

ZooImage is a free (open source) solution for analyzing digital images of zooplankton. In combination with ImageJ, a free image analysis system, it processes digital images, measures individuals, trains for automatic classification of taxa, and finally, measures zooplankton samples (abundances, total and partial size spectra or biomasses, etc.)
