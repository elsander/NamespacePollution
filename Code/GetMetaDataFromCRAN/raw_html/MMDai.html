MMDai: Multivariate Multinomial Distribution Approximation and Imputation for Incomplete Data

Fit incomplete categorical data with infinite mixture of multinomial distribution (Dunson and Xing (2009) <doi:10.1198/jasa.2009.tm08439>). Perform efficient missing data imputation and other statistical inference based on joint distribution estimation.
