DIFtree: Item Focused Trees for the Identification of Items in Differential Item Functioning

Item focused recursive partitioning for simultaneous selection of items and variables that induce Differential Item Functioning (DIF) based on the Rasch Model or the Logistic Regression Approach for DIF detection.
