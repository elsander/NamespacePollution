synchrony: Methods for computing spatial, temporal, and spatiotemporal statistics

Methods for computing spatial, temporal, and spatiotemporal statistics including: empirical univariate, bivariate and multivariate variograms; fitting variogram models; phase locking and synchrony analysis; generating autocorrelated and cross-correlated matrices.
