pollen: Analysis of Aerobiological Data

Supports analysis of aerobiological data. Available features include determination of pollen season limits, replacement of outliers (Kasprzyk and Walanus (2014) <doi:10.1007/s10453-014-9332-8>), and calculation of growing degree days.
