TP.idm: Estimation of Transition Probabilities for the Illness-Death Model

Estimation of transition probabilities for the illness-death model. Both the Aalen-Johansen estimator for a Markov model and a novel non-Markovian estimator by de Una-Alvarez and Meira-Machado (2015) <doi:10.1111/biom.12288> are included.
