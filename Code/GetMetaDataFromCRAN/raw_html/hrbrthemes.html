hrbrthemes: Additional Themes, Theme Components and Utilities for 'ggplot2'

A compilation of extra 'ggplot2' themes, scales and utilities, including a spell check function plot label fields and an overall emphasis on typography. A copy of the 'Google' font 'Roboto Condensed' <https://github.com/google/roboto/> is also included to support one of the typography-oriented themes.
