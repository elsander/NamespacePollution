nonnest2: Tests of Non-Nested Models

Testing non-nested models via theory supplied by Vuong (1989) <doi:10.2307/1912557>. Includes tests of model distinguishability and of model fit that can be applied to both nested and non-nested models. Also includes functionality to obtain confidence intervals associated with AIC and BIC. This material is based on work supported by the National Science Foundation under Grant Number SES-1061334.
