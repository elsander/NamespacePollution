imPois: Imprecise Inference for Poisson Sampling Models

Tools performing an imprecise inference for estimating the parameter of Poisson sampling model. Extended the original work done in the PhD thesis of Lee (2014). The theory of imprecise probabilities introduced by Peter Walley in 1991 becomes the basis of this inferential framework.
