parfm: Parametric Frailty Models

Fits Parametric Frailty Models by maximum marginal likelihood. Possible baseline hazards: Weibull, inverse Weibull, exponential, Gompertz, lognormal and loglogistic. Possible Frailty distributions: gamma, positive stable, inverse Gaussian and lognormal.
