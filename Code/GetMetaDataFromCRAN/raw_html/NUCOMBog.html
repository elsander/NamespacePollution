NUCOMBog: NUtrient Cycling and COMpetition Model Undisturbed Open Bog Ecosystems in a Temperate to Sub-Boreal Climate

Modelling the vegetation, carbon, nitrogen and water dynamics of undisturbed open bog ecosystems in a temperate to sub-boreal climate. The executable of the model can downloaded from <https://github.com/jeroenpullens/source_modelMEE>.
