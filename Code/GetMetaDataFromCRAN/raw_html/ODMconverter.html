ODMconverter: Tools to Convert ODM Files

Transformation of 'ODM' (see <http://www.cdisc.org/odm>) files into R format, Microsoft Office format, 'CDA' format and vice versa. 'ODM' format is commonly used in clinical trials. Semantic annotation (such as 'UMLS', 'SNOMED' or 'LOINC' codes) are transformed accordingly.
