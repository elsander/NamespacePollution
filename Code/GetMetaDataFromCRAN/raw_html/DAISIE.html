DAISIE: Dynamical Assembly of Islands by Speciation, Immigration and Extinction

Simulates and computes the (maximum) likelihood of a dynamical model of island biota assembly through speciation, immigration and extinction. See Valente et al. 2015. Ecology Letters 18: 844-852, <doi:10.1111/ele.12461>.
