eba: Elimination-by-Aspects Models

Fitting and testing multi-attribute probabilistic choice models, especially the Bradley-Terry-Luce (BTL) model (Bradley & Terry, 1952; Luce, 1959), elimination-by-aspects (EBA) models (Tversky, 1972), and preference tree (Pretree) models (Tversky & Sattath, 1979).
