nse: Numerical Standard Errors Computation in R

Collection of functions designed to calculate numerical standard error (NSE) of univariate time series as described in Ardia et al. (2016) <doi:10.2139/ssrn.2741587> and Ardia and Bluteau (2017) <doi:10.2139/ssrn.2741587>.
