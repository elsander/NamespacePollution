colourvision: Colour Vision Models

Colour vision models, colour spaces and colour thresholds. Includes Vorobyev & Osorio Receptor Noise Limited models, Chittka colour hexagon, and Endler & Mielke model. Models have been extended to accept any number of photoreceptor types.
