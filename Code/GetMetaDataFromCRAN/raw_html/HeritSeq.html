HeritSeq: Heritability of Gene Expression for Next-Generation Sequencing

Statistical framework to analyze heritability of gene expression based on next-generation sequencing data and simulating sequencing reads. Variance partition coefficients (VPC) are computed using linear mixed effects and generalized linear mixed effects models. Compound Poisson and negative binomial models are included.
