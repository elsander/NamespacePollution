evaluator: Information Security Quantified Risk Assessment Toolkit

An open source information security strategic risk analysis toolkit based on the OpenFAIR taxonomy <https://www2.opengroup.org/ogsys/catalog/C13K> and risk assessment standard <https://www2.opengroup.org/ogsys/catalog/C13G>. Empowers an organization to perform a quantifiable, repeatable, and data-driven review of its security program.
