hierformR: Analysis of Dynamics Hierarchy Formation

Determine paths and states that social networks develop over time to form social hierarchies. Based upon algorithms described in W. Brent Lindquist & Ivan D. Chase (2009) <doi:10.1007/s11538-008-9371-9>.
