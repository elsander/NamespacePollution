AmostraBrasil: Generates Samples or Complete List of Brazilian IBGE (Instituto Brasileiro De Geografia e Estatistica) Census Households, Geocoding it by Google Maps

Generates samples or complete list of Brazilian IBGE (Instituto Brasileiro de Geografia e Estatistica, see <http://www.ibge.gov.br/> for more information) census households, geocoding it by Google Maps. The package connects IBGE site and downloads maps and census data.
