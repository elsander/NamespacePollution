dfcomb: Phase I/II Adaptive Dose-Finding Design for Combination Studies

Phase I/II adaptive dose-finding design for combination studies. Several methods are proposed depending on the type of combinations: (1) the combination of two cytotoxic agents, and (2) combination of a molecularly targeted agent with a cytotoxic agent.
