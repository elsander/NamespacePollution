multipanelfigure: Infrastructure to Assemble Multi-Panel Figures (from Grobs)

Tools to create a layout for figures made of multiple panels, and to fill the panels with base, lattice and ggplot2 plots, grobs, and PNG, JPEG, SVG and TIFF images.
