EMVC: Entropy Minimization over Variable Clusters

Contains logic for the data-driven optimization of annotations via minimization of the entropy of variable group members over discrete variable clusters (see Frost, HR and Moore, JH (2014) <doi:10.1093/bioinformatics/btu110>).
