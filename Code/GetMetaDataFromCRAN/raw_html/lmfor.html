lmfor: Functions for Forest Biometrics

Functions for different purposes related to Forest biometrics, including illustrative graphics, numerical computation, modeling height-diameter relationships, prediction of tree volumes, and modelling of diameter distributions. Datasets on tree height-diameter relationship, light response of moss photosynthesis, and productivity of stump-lifting machines are included.
