FractalParameterEstimation: Estimation of Parameters p and q for Randomized Sierpinski Carpet for [p-p-p-q]-Model

The parameters p and q are estimated with the aid of randomized Sierpinski Carpet which is built on [p-p-p-q]-model. Thereby, for three times simulation with p-value and once with q-value is assumed. Hence these parameters are estimated and displayed.
