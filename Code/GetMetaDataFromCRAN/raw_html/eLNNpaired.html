eLNNpaired: Model-Based Gene Clustering for Genomics Data from Paired/Matched Designs

Perform model based gene clustering for genomics data generated from paired/matched design based on mixture of extended lognormal normal Bayesian hierarchical models (See Li Y, Morrow J, Raby B, Tantisira K, Weiss ST, Huang W, Qiu W. (2017), <doi:10.1371/journal.pone.0174602>).
