FunCluster: Functional Profiling of Microarray Expression Data

FunCluster performs a functional analysis of microarray expression data based on Gene Ontology & KEGG functional annotations. From expression data and functional annotations FunCluster builds classes of putatively co-regulated biological processes through a specially designed clustering procedure.
