OTUtable: North Temperate Lakes - Microbial Observatory 16S Time Series Data and Functions

Analyses of OTU tables produced by 16S sequencing, as well as example data. It contains the data and scripts used in the paper Linz, et al. (2017) "Bacterial community composition and dynamics spanning five years in freshwater bog lakes" (Manuscript submitted, preprint available at <http://biorxiv.org/content/early/2017/04/12/127035>).
