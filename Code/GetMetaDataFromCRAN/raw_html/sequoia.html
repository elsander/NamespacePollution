sequoia: Pedigree Inference from SNPs

Fast multi-generational pedigree inference from incomplete data on hundreds of SNPs, including parentage assignment and sibship clustering. See article "Pedigree reconstruction from SNP data: Parentage assignment, sibship clustering, and beyond" (Mol Ecol Res, accepted manuscript) for more information.
