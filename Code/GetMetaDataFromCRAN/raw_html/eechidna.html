eechidna: Exploring Election and Census Highly Informative Data Nationally for Australia

Data from the 2013 Australian Federal Election (House of Representatives) and the 2011 Australian Census. Includes tools for visualizing and analysing the data. This package incorporates data that is copyright Commonwealth of Australia (Australian Electoral Commission) 2016.
