dbhydroR: 'DBHYDRO' Hydrologic and Water Quality Data

Client for programmatic access to the South Florida Water Management District's 'DBHYDRO' database at <http://sfwmd.gov/dbhydro>, with functions for accessing hydrologic and water quality data.
