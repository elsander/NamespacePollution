CARBayesdata: Data Used in the Vignettes Accompanying the CARBayes and CARBayesST Packages

Spatio-temporal data from Scotland used in the vignettes accompanying the CARBayes (spatial modelling) and CARBayesST (spatio-temporal modelling) packages. For the CARBayes vignette the data include the Scottish lip cancer data and property price and respiratory hospitalisation data from the Greater Glasgow and Clyde health board. For the CARBayesST vignette the data include spatio-temporal data on property sales and respiratory hospitalisation and air pollution from the Greater Glasgow and Clyde health board.
