JoSAE: Functions for some Unit-Level Small Area Estimators and their Variances

Implementation of some unit level EBLUP and GREG estimators as well as the estimate of their variances to further document the publication of Breidenbach and Astrup (2011). The vignette further explains the use of the implemented functions.
